About the Foundation

Ladidi Baba Foundation is a registered non-profit organization committed to empowering women and youth through education, skills development, and community engagement.

We focus on:

  • Education Support
  • Women Empowerment
  • Youth Skills Development
  • Community Outreach

Our Work in the Community

Our Legal Status

Ladidi Baba Foundation is a duly registered non-profit organization with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria.

Founder’s Message

I founded Ladidi Baba Foundation with a deep passion for empowering women and youth to become confident, skilled, and self-reliant individuals. I believe that when women are empowered and young people are equipped with the right opportunities, communities grow stronger and more sustainable.

Through education support, mentorship, skills development, and community outreach, we are committed to creating lasting impact. My vision is to see women thrive, youth lead with confidence, and communities transformed through opportunity and support.

— Founder, Ladidi Baba Foundation
