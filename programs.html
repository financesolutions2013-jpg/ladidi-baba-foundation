Our Programs

Our programs are designed to equip women and youth with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need to thrive economically and socially.

Economic Empowerment

Skills training, entrepreneurship support, and financial literacy programs.

Education & ICT Development

Digital skills training, scholarships, and mentorship programs.

Advocacy & Rights Protection

Gender equality campaigns and support services.

Economic Empowerment & Entrepreneurship

  • Vocational Skills Training: Hands-on training in tailoring, catering, ICT, etc.
  • Business Support: Access to grants and mentorship.
  • Financial Literacy: Budgeting and investment education.
  • Scholarships: Financial support for vulnerable students.
  • Gender-Based Violence (GBV) Campaigns:Sensitization on women's rights, reproductive health, and psychological support for survivors of abuse.
  • Civic Engagement: Training in leadership, governance, and peacebuilding to encourage participation in community decision-making.
  • Widows Support Initiative: Advocacy and social services to alleviate the burdens faced by widows.
  • Sports for Development:Using athletics to teach life skills, teamwork, and improve physical and mental health among youth.
